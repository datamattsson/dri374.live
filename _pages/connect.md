# What's in a name?

DRI374 [diː ɑːr aɪ θriː ˈsɛvᵊnti fɔː] is a unique channel name not be confused with anything else out there. The moniker is a play on high score initials and the numerical representation of D-R-I (look at your phone keypad). The number 374 was also the motocross rider number assigned to the same in a Swedish amateur/veteran racing circuit. At the time (early 2000), many riders created their socials with their initials and race number, DRI374 is simply just that, with the uptick that it is universally unique.

Raccoon City Pinball is the name of the home arcade that stuck after having raccoons running on the garage roof in Redwood City and having a friend misheard Redwood City as Raccoon City. While certain flirtations are made in true counterfeit manner with Resident Evil or Rocket from Guardians of the Galaxy, they are mainly for shits and giggles when it lends itself for the situation. I have not played Resident Evil since RE4 and I'm not really a Marvel fan at all.
